Evolution

Our purpose is to become the central hub for all musical entertainment in the orange Dynasty. From hosting Karaoke spaces to Singing competitions. As well as helping upcoming Artistes to find their places and getting the recognition they deserve. We hope to grow into a full record label under SIGN. We Also have plans in place as well as a roadmap to follow to achieve these goals. From Spotlighting artistes to NFT implementation to Vocal Training and more.
